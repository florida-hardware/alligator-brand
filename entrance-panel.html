Entrance Panel

Our entrance panel with gate is built to stand rigid for ease of opening and closing when handling livestock. All of entrance panels stand 8’ high for easy clearance and are available in 4’ or 6’ lengths. These panels feature 6 bar, 1 ¾” rounded tubing and stand 63” high.
